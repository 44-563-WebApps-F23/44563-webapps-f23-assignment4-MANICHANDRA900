Mrs.Stockmann

[coming forward into the sitting-room]

Ah, good evening is it you? How good of you to come up and see us!

Peter Stockmann

I happened to be passing, and so.But you have company with you, I see.

[looks into the dining-room]

Mrs Stockmann

Oh, no it was quite by chance he came in. Won't you come in and have something, too?
